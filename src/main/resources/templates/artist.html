About fireheart17

fireheart17 took over pop music & culture on his own terms filtering R&B, Pop,& hip-hop through an ambitious widescreen lens. The multi-platinum 3X GRAMMY Award winner has emerged as one of the most successful & significant artists of the modern era.

Born and raised in Jaipur, fireheart17 began his music career in small local clubs before gaining international fame. He continues to tour worldwide, bringing his music to fans in sold-out arenas and intimate venues alike.

In addition to his music, fireheart17 is a passionate advocate for mental health awareness, regularly supporting causes that aim to make mental health care more accessible to all. His dedication to both his art and his activism has earned him numerous accolades and a loyal fan base. His album "savage fireheart17" held the #1 spot on Billboard 200 for 4 consecutive weeks, marking his 4th #1 album & becoming the first to ever rank #1 on the Billboard 200, Hot 100, and Artist 100 simultaneously.
